to catch the sounds that she can then give back with her own voice, 2026, zine, 8.5" x 5.5"

This zine collects a description of the project by the artist, the score and script from the 2021 performance excerpted in the video, and answers from 22 practitioners across disciplines to the question, “How do you think about repetition in your work?”
